Crystal Collector!!

Instructions:

You will be given a random number at the begining of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific number of points to your total score.

You win by matching your total score to the random number; you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the value of each crystal.
